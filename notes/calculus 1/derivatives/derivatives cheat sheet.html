Derivatives

The derivative of $f(x)$ gives a function $f'(x)$

$f'(x)$ gives the slope of the tangent line, or the rate of change at $x$

Limit Defintion

$$\begin{align} f'(x) = \lim_{h \to 0}\frac{f(x + h) - f(x)}{h} \\ f'(a) = \lim_{x \to a}\frac{f(x) - f(a)}{x - a} \end{align}$$

Common Derivatives

Assume $c$ is a constant

$$\begin{align} \begin{array}{| c | c | c | c |} \hline \frac{d}{dx} c = 0 & \frac{d}{dx} x = 1 & \frac{d}{dx} x^2 = 2x & \frac{d}{dx} \sqrt{x} = \frac{1}{2\sqrt{x}} \\ \hline \frac{d}{dx} x^2 = 2x & \frac{d}{dx} sin(x) = cos(x) & \frac{d}{dx} cos(x) = -sin(x) & \frac{d}{dx} tan(x) = sec^2(x) \\ \hline \frac{d}{dx} cot(x) = -csc^2(x) & \frac{d}{dx} sec(x) = sec(x)tan(x) & \frac{d}{dx} csc(x) = -csc(x)cot(x) & \frac{d}{dx} sin^{-1}(x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} \\ \hline \frac{d}{dx} cos^{-1}(x) = -\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} & \frac{d}{dx} tan^{-1}(x) = \frac{1}{x^2 + 1} & \frac{d}{dx} cot^{-1}(x) = -\frac{1}{x^2 + 1} & \frac{d}{dx} sec^{-1}(x) = \frac{1}{|x|\sqrt{x^2 - 1}} \\ \hline \frac{d}{dx} csc^{-1}(x) = -\frac{1}{|x|\sqrt{x^2 - 1}} & \frac{d}{dx} c^x = c^xln(c) & \frac{d}{dx} e^x = e^x & \frac{d}{dx} ln(x) = \frac{1}{x} \\ \hline \frac{d}{dx} log_c(x) = \frac{1}{ln(c)x} & \frac{d}{dx} sinh(x) = cosh(x) & \frac{d}{dx} cosh(x) = sinh(x) & \frac{d}{dx} tanh(x) = sech^2(x) \\ \hline \frac{d}{dx} coth(x) = -csch^2(x) & \frac{d}{dx} sech(x) = -sech(x)tanh(x) & \frac{d}{dx} csch(x) = -csch(x)coth(x) & \frac{d}{dx} sinh^{-1}(x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} \\ \hline \frac{d}{dx} cosh^{-1}(x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} & \frac{d}{dx} tanh^{-1}(x) = \frac{1}{1 - x^2} & \frac{d}{dx} coth^{-1}(x) = -\frac{1}{1 - x^2} & \frac{d}{dx} sech^{-1}(x) = \frac{1}{|x|\sqrt{1-x^2}} \\ \hline \frac{d}{dx} csch^{-1}(x) = -\frac{1}{|x|\sqrt{1 + x^2}} & \frac{d}{dx} y = \frac{dy}{dx} & \frac{d}{dx} z = \frac{dz}{dx} & \frac{d}{dx} t = \frac{dt}{dx} \\ \hline \end{array} \end{align}$$

Derivative Properties

For the following, assume $f'(x)$ and $g'(x)$ exist and $c$ is a constant

Constant Coefficiant Property:

$$\begin{align} \frac{d}{dx} cf(x) = cf'(x) \end{align}$$

Sum/Difference Property:

$$\begin{align} \frac{d}{dx} f(x) \pm g(x) = f'(x) \pm g'(x) \end{align}$$

Power Rule:

$$\begin{align} \frac{d}{dx} x^c = cx^{c-1} \end{align}$$

Product Rule:

$$\begin{align} \frac{d}{dx} f(x)g(x) = f'(x)g(x) + g'(x)f(x) \end{align}$$

Quotient Rule:

$$\begin{align} \frac{d}{dx} \frac{f(x)}{g(x)} = \frac{f'(x)g(x) - g'(x)f(x)}{g(x)^2} \end{align}$$

Chain Rule:

$$\begin{align} \frac{d}{dx} f(g(x)) = g'(x)f'(g(x)) \end{align}$$
